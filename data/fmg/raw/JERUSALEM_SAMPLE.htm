**GODFREY OF BOUILLON**, son of EUSTACHE II Comte de Boulogne & his wife Ida of Lorraine (-Jerusalem 18 Jul 1100, bur Church of the Holy Sepulchre). Duke of Lower Lotharingia. He joined the First Crusade in 1096 and was elected Guardian of the Holy Sepulchre in 1099. The necrology of Argenteuil records the death "XV Kal Aug" of "Godefridus rex Iherosolimitanus". William of Tyre records his death and burial.

**BAUDOUIN I** de Boulogne, son of EUSTACHE II Comte de Boulogne & his wife Ida of Lorraine ([1058]-el-Aarisch 2 Apr 1118, bur Church of the Holy Sepulchre). King of Jerusalem. He joined the First Crusade with his brother Godefroy. Married to **Morphia of Melitene**, daughter of Gabriel of Melitene.

**BAUDOUIN II** du Bourg, son of HUGUES [I] Comte de Rethel & his wife Mélisinde de Montlhéry (-Jerusalem 21 Aug 1131, bur Church of the Holy Sepulchre). Count of Edessa 1100-1118. King of Jerusalem 1118. Married to **Morfia**, daughter of Gabriel Lord of Melitene. Brother of Gervais Patriarch of Reims.

**MÉLISENDE** de Jérusalem, daughter of BAUDOUIN II King of Jerusalem & his wife Morfia of Melitene (1105-Jerusalem 11 Sep 1161, bur Church of the Holy Sepulchre). Queen of Jerusalem. She succeeded her father as Queen. Married to **Foulques V** "le Jeune" Comte d'Anjou. Mother of Baudouin III and Amaury I.

**BAUDOUIN III** de Jérusalem, son of FULQUES V "le Jeune" Comte d'Anjou & his wife Mélisende Queen of Jerusalem (1130-Tripoli, Lebanon 10 Feb 1162, bur Church of the Holy Sepulchre). King of Jerusalem. He succeeded his mother in 1152. Married to **Théodora Comnène**, niece of Emperor Manuel I. Died without legitimate issue.

**AMAURY I** de Jérusalem, son of FULQUES V "le Jeune" Comte d'Anjou & his wife Mélisende Queen of Jerusalem (1136-Jerusalem 11 Jul 1174, bur Church of the Holy Sepulchre). King of Jerusalem. He succeeded his brother. Married firstly to **Agnès de Courtenay**. Married secondly to **Marie Comnène**. Father of Baudouin IV le Lépreux and Sibylle.

**BAUDOUIN IV** "le Lépreux" de Jérusalem, son of AMAURY I King of Jerusalem & his first wife Agnès de Courtenay (1161-Jerusalem 16 Mar 1185, bur Church of the Holy Sepulchre). King of Jerusalem. He succeeded his father despite suffering from leprosy. Died unmarried and without legitimate issue.

**SIBYLLE** de Jérusalem, daughter of AMAURY I King of Jerusalem & his first wife Agnès de Courtenay (1159-Acre Jul 1190, bur Acre). Queen of Jerusalem. She succeeded her brother. Married to **Guy de Lusignan**. Mother of Baudouin V.

**BAUDOUIN V** de Lusignan, son of GUI de Lusignan King of Jerusalem & his wife Sibylle Queen of Jerusalem (1177/78-Acre Aug/Sep 1186, bur Church of the Holy Sepulchre). King of Jerusalem. He succeeded his uncle under the regency of Raymond III Comte de Tripoli. Died in childhood.

**ISABELLE I** de Jérusalem, daughter of AMAURY I King of Jerusalem & his second wife Marie Comnène (1172-Naples 5 Apr 1205, bur Naples). Queen of Jerusalem. She succeeded her half-sister. Married four times: Humphrey IV de Toron, Conrad de Montferrat, Henri II de Champagne, and Amaury II de Lusignan.
